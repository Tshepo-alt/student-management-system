Privacy Notice

Your data is respected, protected, and never misused.

Effective date: 27 April 2026

GIPS College (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Student Portal. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information. By using the portal, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

  • Personal information – full name, student number, email address, phone number, physical/postal address, date of birth, nationality, ID/passport number, emergency contact details.
  • Academic information – program, courses, grades, attendance, exam registrations, accommodation requests.
  • Financial information – payment history, receipts, sponsorship status.
  • Technical information – IP address, browser type, device information, cookies.
  • Communications – messages sent via the portal (queries, tickets, chatbot conversations).

2. How we use your information

  • To manage your student account, enrolment, examinations, and payments.
  • To communicate important updates (registration deadlines, exam schedules, admission decisions).
  • To provide online classes (Google Meet) and AI-powered chatbot support.
  • To improve our services and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Third‑party services

We use the following trusted services that may process your data:

  • Google Meet / Calendar – for online classes (meeting links are generated using your course and email).
  • OpenAI API – to power the chatbot (conversations are anonymised where possible).
  • Email service (SMTP) – to send you notifications (e.g., password reset, payment confirmation).
  • Cloud database (Aiven) – hosted securely in the cloud.

4. Data retention

Your data is kept as long as you are a student or alumni, and for up to 5 years after graduation for academic record purposes. You may request deletion earlier by contacting the Registrar.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal or academic record‑keeping obligations).
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., for cookies or marketing emails).

To exercise these rights, email IT Support at it@gipscollege.edu.bw.

6. Security

We protect your data using encryption (SSL), secure cookies, and role‑based access controls. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login sessions and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some portal features may not work.

8. Changes to this notice

Any updates will be posted on this page. The “Last updated” date will be changed accordingly.
