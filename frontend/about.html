Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible, and transformative education that empowers students to become innovative leaders, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of higher learning in Africa, recognized globally for academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and holistic student development.

Our History

Founded in 1991, GIPS College began as a small computer training center in Gaborone. Over three decades, we have grown into a multi‑campus institution offering diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate programs across Business, Information Technology, Education, Health Sciences, and Engineering. Our journey reflects a steadfast commitment to innovation, quality, and student success.

Our Campuses & Facilities

We operate three modern campuses designed to foster learning, collaboration, and well‑being:

  • Main Campus (Gaborone) – Our flagship campus featuring state‑of‑the‑art libraries, science labs, a 500‑seat auditorium, sports complex, and on‑site student accommodation.
  • City Campus (Francistown) – Located in the city centre, focused on business, law, and IT programs with modern lecture halls and a digital resource centre.
  • Virtual Campus (Online) – A fully digital learning environment offering live classes, recorded lectures, and 24/7 access to course materials, accessible from anywhere.

Core Values

  • Excellence – We strive for the highest academic standards and continuous improvement.
  • Integrity – We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical conduct.
  • Innovation – We embrace technology and modern pedagogies to enhance learning.
  • Inclusivity – We celebrate diversity and provide equal opportunities for all.
  • Community Engagement – We partner with industry, government, and civil society to create impact.

Leadership

GIPS College is governed by a Board of Directors and led by an experienced executive team comprising the Vice‑Chancellor, Registrar, Dean of Academics, Dean of Students, and Finance Director. Our leaders bring decades of experience in education, administration, and industry, ensuring strategic direction and operational excellence.

Student Life & Support

Beyond academics, we offer a vibrant campus life with student clubs (sports, arts, entrepreneurship, tech), career fairs, mentorship programs, and counselling services. Our 24/7 chatbot support and helpdesk ensure that you always have assistance when needed.

Accreditation & Partnerships

GIPS College is accredited by the Botswana Qualifications Authority (BQA) and is a member of the Association of African Universities. We have established partnerships with industry leaders such as Google, Microsoft, Moodle, and Stripe, as well as academic exchange programs with universities in South Africa, the UK, and India.

Our Commitment to You

We are committed to your success. From the moment you enrol, you become part of the GIPS family. We provide personalised academic advising, career services, internship placements, and alumni networking to help you achieve your goals.
